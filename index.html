Welcome!

Esports 101


My website is going to be a site where people can access information regarding Esports tournaments and news. It’ll hold different pages where you can see recent tournament wins for videogames like CS:GO, Overwatch, League of Legends, Hearthstone, and a few others. I’ll have match scores for different games listed on another page too. I’m hoping to have small articles of recent news on the homepage, detailing recent events in the industry. Whether it’s exciting tournament victories, player trades, or even recent drama I hope to capture a little bit of everything regarding Esports on my website. My goal for the site is to allow people a chance to view all things Esports in condensed, bit size version. I hope to target those who are either new to the Esports industry or maybe those who are veterans of this community. It’ll almost be a type ESPN for professional video games that will allow those who are unfamiliar a chance to peak inside what this community is like. I hope to embed videos or small clips of exciting moments during professional matches in all types of games.
